Rice Pilaf Recipe

Description

A delicious and aromatic rice dish that's perfect as a side dish or main course.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat
  2. Saut√© onions until translucent (about 5 minutes)
  3. Add garlic and rice, cook until rice is lightly toasted
  4. Pour in broth, bring to a boil
  5. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for 18-20 minutes
  6. Let stand for 5 minutes, then fluff with fork
