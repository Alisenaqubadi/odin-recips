Chelo Kabab

Description

Chelo Kabab is a classic Persian dish consisting of grilled meat kababs served with steamed saffron rice (chelo).

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 medium onion, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Saffron (a pinch)
  • Butter for serving

Steps

  1. Mix the ground meat with grated onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and turmeric
  2. Shape the meat mixture into long kababs around skewers
  3. Cook the basmati rice with saffron until fluffy
  4. Grill the kababs until well-cooked
  5. Serve the kababs over the rice with a pat of butter
